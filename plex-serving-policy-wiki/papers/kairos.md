_Editorial paraphrase, not the paper's verbatim abstract._

The proposed policy centers on context-aware concurrency admission, routing, and dvfs. The reported evaluation context includes SWE-bench Verified, DABStep, Terminal-Bench 2.0, mini-swe-agent, among other workloads.
