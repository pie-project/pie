## Abstract synopsis

_Editorial paraphrase, not the paper's verbatim abstract._

This work studies request ordering, token allocation, fairness, admission, or latency-SLO control in shared inference serving. The proposed policy centers on interaction-aware throttling and weighted service. The reported evaluation context includes Microsoft Copilot production traces across 34 applications.
